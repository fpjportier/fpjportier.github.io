I am a Professor of Economics at [University College London](http://www.ucl.ac.uk/economics), on unpaid leave from [Toulouse School of Economics](http://www.tse-fr.eu/), [Université Toulouse 1 Capitole](http://www.univ-tlse1.fr). I am also an Honorary Senior Member of Institut Universitaire de France.

My research focuses on macroeconomics, business cycles, monetary policy, and the role of expectations in economic fluctuations.
